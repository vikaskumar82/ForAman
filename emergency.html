EMERGENCY


What should I do in an event of a dental emergency?

Seville Dental is open from 9:30 am but our phone lines are open every day including weekends and public holidays. So, please give us a call any time so that we can help you in making the appointment as quickly as possible.

What should I do to cure a toothache happening after hours?

If you are suffering from a painful toothache, something is wrong that requires attention. Please contact Seville Dental immediately to organize an appointment as quickly and conveniently as possible for you. Pain killers such as Panadol or Nurofen are recommended. Do not exceed the maximum dose.

What should I do if I have a swelling?

If swelling(s) on face/neck are drastically increasing, contact nearest Emergengy/Hospital/Ambulance.

What should I do if I have chipped my tooth?

Fractured teeth can usually be restored so there is no need to panic. If the tooth is painful, avoid hot or cold and avoid eating or biting on that side i.e. relieve the tooth from extremes of temperature and pressure. Painkillers such as Ibuprofen or Paracetamol may help.

What if I have lost a crown, bridge or veneers?

Keep your crown, bridge or veneers safe so it can be re-cemented as soon as possible to avoid damage.

What if I have knocked a tooth out?

The tooth needs to be re-implanted into its socket immediately. Try not to handle the tooth root to protect the fibres around the root surface. If possible, DO NOT place the tooth in water. DO place the tooth in cold milk. Contact us on : (03) 5964 4340.

What if I have broken my denture?

Broken dentures can usually be repaired relatively easily. Please resist the temptation to glue them together your self as this often makes a repair very difficult. Contact us on : (03) 5964 4340.
